Introduction



The app "FSYourTime" is designed for students of Frankfurt School of Finance & Management and aims to support the manual time tracking of students’ individual workload. Every module with 6 ECTS points is calculated to have a workload of 150 hours in summary. These 150 hours of workload are composed of three different effort categories: attendance in lectures, self-learning time and exam preparation. The two latter categories are further divided into subcategories which will be described later on.

Consequently, the tracked workload should only include the time a student needs to prepare and follow up a certain module. This results in an overview of students’ actual workload for each module and can give Frankfurt School of Finance & Management a guideline for future module conception.

Short overview of app functions



Live tracking


• Enables you to book your effort while actually working
• Select your class and type of effort (--> Look at Selection of module & effort type)
• Just click on “Start” when you start your work for the selected module
• If you are finished with you work, click on “Stop”
• The app will show you the start and end time
• Click on “Submit” (right corner of the menu) to save your effort
• Click on “Reset” to reset the timer and start again

New effort


• Enables you subsequent booking of effort which you could not track live
• Select your class and type of effort (--> Look at Selection of module & effort type)
• Select the date, start time and end time and click on “Save”
• The app will save your effort for the selected module automatically
• Note: You cannot select a date more than 14 days in the past!

Change booked effort


• You can subsequently change and delete your booked effort for the different modules
• Note: You cannot change effort for a date more than 14 days in the past!


Selection of module & effort type

Pick your class


• The individual courses will be loaded automatically for every student
• Select the course for which you want to book effort


Type of effort


• Options: “Exam preparation“, “Lecture preparation”, “Homework”, “Presentation”, “Paper”, “Group work”
• Select the respective type of effort for your module
